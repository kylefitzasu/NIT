🏌 Format

Style: Modified Scotch  ·  Max Handicap Differential: 12 strokes

  • One player tees off on odd-numbered holes; the other on even-numbered holes
  • Partners alternate shots from there throughout the hole
⛳ Tee Boxes
  • Official tees: Blue
  • Other tees may be played only if all four players agree
  • Any dispute defaults to Blue Tees
🚫 Match Rules
  • No ties — play extra holes until a winner is determined
  • Both teams still earn points in extra-hole matches
  • Winning team must submit results via their bracket's Google Form
No-shows: Both no-show → loss for both  ·  One no-show → forfeit win for attending team
📊 Team Handicaps

Dynamic handicaps — use your GHIN index from the day of the match.

  1. Add both players' GHIN indexes and divide by 2 to get the team handicap
  2. Use the Blue Tees Course Handicap (via the GHIN app)
  3. Higher-handicap team receives the difference in strokes, applied to the #1 and #2 handicap holes on the front 9 and the #1 handicap hole on the back 9
Example:
Team A: 21 ÷ 2 = 10.5 → rounds to 11
Team B: 16 ÷ 2 = 8
Strokes given: 11 − 8 = 3 (front #1 & #2, back #1 handicap holes)
🔄 Substitutes
  • Substitutes must have a valid GHIN index
  • A sub's handicap may be higher or lower than the replaced player
  • If no eligible sub is found before match deadline, the match is forfeited
  • A player who started the season on one team may not sub for another team

🎯 Playoff Qualification

Top 10 teams qualify for playoffs at season's end.

  1. Automatic (4): Bracket winner from each of the 4 brackets
  2. Wild cards (6): Next 6 highest-scoring teams across all brackets
  3. Seeding: Ranked by total points; bracket winners guaranteed entry
  4. Tiebreakers: W/L record → head-to-head → 3-hole playoff → coin flip
